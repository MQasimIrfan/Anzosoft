History

Anzosoft Journey

2020

Anzosoft Officially Founded

Anzosoft was established with the mission to deliver scalable, intelligent, and future-ready software solutions. With a core focus on innovation and impact, our founding team brought together years of industry experience across data engineering, mobile apps, and full-stack development.

2024

Launched Core Engineering Services

In 2024, we officially launched our full range of services including Web Development, DevOps Engineering, and Data Analytics. Our teams began delivering solutions using modern frameworks like React, Vue, Terraform, Kubernetes, and Power BI for a diverse set of clients across industries.

2025

Expanding into Mobile & BI Solutions

By 2025, Anzosoft expanded its offerings into cross-platform mobile app development using Flutter and React Native. We also built custom Business Intelligence dashboards to help clients make data-driven decisions. Our client base grew globally, and our team expanded to include more specialized engineers and analysts.
